About

Nine years on the ground, before any of this.

Nayan J Kalita is a serial founder, speaker, and impact builder based in Guwahati, Assam. With a B.E. from Gauhati University and an M.Tech in Information Technology from Tezpur University, his journey started not with a venture - but with a magazine.

In 2012, he founded Xondhan (xondhan.com) - one of Assam's first Unicode Assamese e-magazines, launched to bring the language and its diaspora together online. It became the first Assamese e-magazine to introduce video interviews, drew readers from over 25 countries, and won the Manthan South East Asia & Asia Pacific Award 2015 (e-Culture & Heritage) and the eNorth East Award 2016. That early experiment, proving that Assamese cultural content could reach the world, shaped everything that followed.

Today, he leads four ventures: The Chayi (Assam tea & kombucha, funded by BIRAC BIG, RKVY RAFTAAR), Dzucha Brew Lab (cocktail mixers), Khyontek AI (AI for underserved communities), and Aarhi Impact Foundation (rural women's entrepreneurship across NE India). His motto: building from Assam's roots.

None of this happens solo - it's built with teams, tea garden partners, and the women entrepreneurs Aarhi works alongside.
