About

Nine years on the ground, before any of this.

Nayan J Kalita is a serial founder, speaker, and impact builder based in Guwahati, Assam. With a B.E. from Gauhati University and an M.Tech in Information Technology from Tezpur University, he spent nine years leading Xondhan Foundation's rural community development programs across Northeast India before pivoting to entrepreneurship.

Today, he leads four ventures: The Chayi (Assam tea & kombucha, bootstrapped to ₹35L+ revenue), Dzucha Brew Lab (cocktail mixers & hard kombucha), Khyontek AI (AI research for underserved communities), and Aarhi Impact Foundation (rural women's entrepreneurship). His motto: building from Assam's roots.

None of this happens solo — it's built with teams, tea garden partners, and the women entrepreneurs Aarhi works alongside.
